**Atlas Shrugged**. The book is set in a dystopian United States on the
brink of economic collapse. Exhausted by a corrupt government, the hero,
John Galt, packs his things and starts a self-sufficient community in an
isolated valley, hidden - and separate - from the U.S. He recruits
the nation's business leaders to quit their jobs and populate his
utopia.

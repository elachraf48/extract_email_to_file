g agent, the same position the company claimed to have eliminated.

=A0 =A0 =A0 =A0 =A0 Such alleged conduct violates the Age Discrimination =
in Employment Act (ADEA), which prohibits discrimination against individu=

00") after first attempting to reach a pre-litigation settlement through =
its conciliation process.
